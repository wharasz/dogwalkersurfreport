Friday, April 18 · 7:14 AM
Knee-to-waist with occasional sets — glassy early, SE wind filling in
Walked down at first light with the dog. Glassy and clean, sets coming in around waist high with decent shape on the peaks north of the pier. The outside bar is showing some potential if the swell ticks up this afternoon. Wind was dead calm at check — that SE onshore is forecast to kick in around 10am so get out early. Water is warm, no wetsuit needed. Boil was visible on the horizon from last night's launch.
